CHRISTOPHER LOCK

PORTFOLIO OF SELECTED WORKS

LuChen FW22 Runway Show:

My role: Composer, Producer, Performer, Recording Engineer

In the Spring of 2022 I produced and performed live electronic music for LuChen's FW22 Runway show during New York Fashion Week. The live set involved performing in Ableton Live with several midi controllers, synchronized to lighting/choreography cues. The show was positively reviewed by Vogue Magazine, Fashion Week Magazine, and other news outlets.

Moel Y Gaer, Bodfari:

My role: Composer, Producer, Data Sonification, Documentor

This piece was concieved by transforming diagrams and maps from an archeological excavation site at Moel Y Gaer, Bodfari into a graphical, animated score. Read more here.

Intona.AI:

My role: Software Developer, Composer, Producer, Recording Engineer

Since August of 2023 I have been working on an AI based sound design tool and software instrument called Intona.AI. Intona was trained on a database of sounds made up from compositions that I have created over the past 5 years, during my time as part of the Harvard Group For New Music, and partially serves as a kind of living portfilio somwhat representitive of my compositional soundworld. Read more here.
